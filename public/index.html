⚠️ Emergency Access (Lockout Recovery)

If you ever lock yourself out due to IP restriction, you cannot fix it from this page (you'd need to be logged in already). Instead, use your Render Shell:

# Step 1 — disable IP check (run in Render Shell)
touch data/bypass.txt
# Step 2 — after fixing IPs in Settings, remove the bypass
rm data/bypass.txt

To open Render Shell: go to render.com → your service → click Shell in the left sidebar. Only you can do this — it requires your Render account login. No one else can bypass IP restriction this way.
